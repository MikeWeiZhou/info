# Recipe: Soy Suace Steamed Fish

## Ingredients
- fish
- liquid sauce
  - chicken broth (1/2 cup)
  - soy sauce (2 tblsp)
  - oyster sauce (1 tblsp)
  - salt + sugar
  - dash of sesame oil
  - *chinese cooking wine (hua diao jiu)*
- *dried* shitaki mushrooms
- ginger (quarter cup ginger)
- garlic (3 cloves)
- *chinese green onions*

## Instructions (roughly 15 mins including prep)
1. [Steam fish for 7-8 minutes](https://youtu.be/zC8ra20Wz68?t=364)
2. [Saute sauce while fish steaming](https://youtu.be/zC8ra20Wz68?t=167)
    - saute garlic, ginger, and shitaki musthrooms till garlic starts browning
    - add all liquid sauce ingredients, bring to boil and simmer 30 seconds
3. [Add sauce to fish](https://youtu.be/zC8ra20Wz68?t=410)
    - pour excess liquid from fish dish
    - add chinese green onions to sauce
    - pour sauce on to fish
